Privacy Policy – CVPilot

Last updated: April 2026
App: CVPilot
Developer / Publisher: CAREO LIMITED
Contact: careolimited@gmail.com

CVPilot (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Policy explains what information is processed, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data Processing Overview

CVPilot is designed to operate fully on-device.
No user data is sent to external servers
No backend, API, or cloud database is used
All processing happens locally on your device
We do not collect personal data such as email, name, or account information.

2. Locally Stored Data (On-Device Only)

The app uses local storage (AsyncStorage) to function properly. This data never leaves your device.

Stored data may include:

  • Resume (CV) content (last used or cached)
  • Job description text (last used)
  • Draft CV data (builder feature)
  • Analysis results and history
  • Usage limits (e.g. free analysis quota)
  • Subscription status (free / premium)
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored only to:

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Allow offline usage

3. Processing Purposes

All data is processed locally for:

  • Resume analysis and scoring
  • Matching CV with job descriptions
  • Generating improvement suggestions
  • Saving analysis history
  • Managing usage limits and premium access

CVPilot uses a deterministic analysis engine, not a remote AI service. No external processing occurs.

4. Subscriptions & Payments

CVPilot offers optional premium subscriptions.

Payments are handled securely by:

  • Google Play Billing (Android)
  • Apple In-App Purchases (iOS)

We do not store or access payment details
Subscription status may be cached locally for app behavior

5. Advertising & Tracking

No ads are displayed
No advertising identifiers (IDFA / AD_ID) are used
No tracking or profiling is performed
No third-party advertising SDKs are integrated

6. Third-Party Services

CVPilot does not use external APIs, analytics tools, or cloud services.
All functionality runs locally within the app.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.
Because no data is transmitted externally, there is nothing to share.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device
Data remains until:

  • You delete it manually (if applicable), or
  • You uninstall the app

Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

9. User Rights

Since no personal data is collected or stored externally:
There is no server-side data to access, modify, or delete
You have full control via your device

10. Data Security

All processing occurs locally on your device
No data transmission over the internet
No external storage or exposure risk

11. Children’s Privacy

CVPilot is intended for users aged 13 and above.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

12. International Data Transfer

No data is transferred internationally, as no data leaves your device.
